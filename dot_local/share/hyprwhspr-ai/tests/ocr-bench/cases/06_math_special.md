### Pythagorean theorem

In a right triangle, the squared length of the hypotenuse equals the sum of the squared lengths of the other two sides:

a² + b² = c²

### Bayes' rule

P(A | B) = P(B | A) · P(A) / P(B)

### Common symbols

- Quantifiers: ∀ (for all), ∃ (there exists), ∄ (does not exist)
- Set theory: ∈ (in), ∉ (not in), ⊂ ⊆ (subset), ∪ ∩ (union, intersection), ∅ (empty set)
- Comparison: ≤ ≥ ≠ ≈ ≡ (≡ is identical to)
- Greek constants: π ≈ 3.14159, e ≈ 2.71828, φ ≈ 1.61803
- Operators: ∑ (sum), ∏ (product), ∫ (integral), √ (root), ∂ (partial), ∇ (nabla)
- Arrows: → ← ↔ ⇒ ⇐ ⇔ ↦ ⟶

### An inequality

∀ x ∈ ℝ : x² ≥ 0
