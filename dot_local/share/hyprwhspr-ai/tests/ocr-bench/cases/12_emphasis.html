Inline-formatting stress test

This paragraph mixes bold weight, italic slant, bold-italic together, strikethrough, and underline. Inline code like parse_chandra_to_markdown(raw) appears next to emphasized prose.

Math notation in prose: H2O, CO2, and the equation E = mc2. Also matrices like Aij, exponents like 210 = 1024, and footnote markers[1].

Mixed inline formatting: important values like temperature=0.0 and max_tokens=4096 are load-bearing. Old defaults have been replaced — see section 3.2 for details.

[1] Footnote: this stress test exercises bold, italic, strike, underline, inline-code, superscript, and subscript in the same flow.
